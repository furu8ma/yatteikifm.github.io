We are uncovering better way out of Yatteiki by doing it and helping others do it. Through this work we have come to value:

Actions over will
Products over processes
Practices over one-hit wonder
Individuals over society
Heroes over Mobs

That is, while there is value in the items on the right, we value the items on the left more.
